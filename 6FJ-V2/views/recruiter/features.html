Features

The “War for Talent” is competitive & time-sensitive. Your search and connection with candidates needs to be quick & painless!

View Pricing & Packages

Contact Candidates Directly

Recruiters love the fact that we provide the required contact information needed for them to reach out to candidates. This is a major pain point for recruiters when using other competitive services. We also pose no barriers of communication - nor do we force you to use an internal messaging system.

Manually Pre-Screened Candidates

Our membership committee pre-screens (by hand) each job seeker that registers for our community. This includes a required profile, valid resume, contact information, and $100K+ salary history. Our pre-screening policy is a proven principle that 6FigureJobs has always upheld to ensure a quality product.

Complete Profiles

All candidates in the 6FigureJobs community have complete profiles which enables recruiters to thoroughly search for and evaluate a candidate’s qualifications and experience.

Free Service for Job Seekers

The fact that we are a free career site for job seekers allows recruiters to reach an active and/or passive audience of talent. Recruiters do not have to wonder if a candidate will have the ability to apply to a job or even be contacted by them.

Targeted Search Functionality

Our search technology enables recruiters to find the talent they need for a specific position quickly and efficiently.

Experienced Talent Specialists

Our talent specialists have vast recruitment experience. They work directly with clients and know the candidates in our system better than anyone. Having a hands-on team to support your recruiting strategies and to deliver the qualified candidates you expect to receive gives you a tremendous advantage.
